Mac and Cheese

Description

Delicious, traditional, and cheesy mac and cheese to travel back to childhood.

Ingredients

  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Bring pot of water to boil. Cook elbow macaroni for 8 minutes.
  2. At the same time, melt butter over medium heat. Add flour and salt and stit for a couple of minutes. Pour milk slowly while stirring.
  3. Add Cheddar cheese and stir until melted.
  4. Drain macaroni and fold into sauce.
  5. Enjoy!
