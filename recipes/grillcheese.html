Grilled Cheese

Description

Delicious, traditional, and cheesy Grilled cheese to travel back to childhood.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread.
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat pan to medium heat. Butter one side of each bread.
  2. Add slices of cheese to one slice and cover with the other slice.
  3. Cook on the pan until golden brown and melty
  4. Enjoy!
